24-HR SCHEDULE
The timeline of the verse
Day 1 — 09:00 AM

Registrations & Check-In

Teams arrive, register on-site, collect kits and get ready to enter the CypherVerse.

Day 1 — 10:00 AM

Opening Ceremony

Welcome address by DevHive Club, track announcements, and problem statement reveal. Let the chaos begin.

Day 1 — 11:00 AM

Hacking Begins 🚀

The 24-hour countdown starts. Teams dive into building their ideas from scratch.

Day 1 — 02:00 PM

Mentor Sessions

Industry mentors circulate to guide teams, offer feedback, and help unblock creative challenges.

Day 1 — 08:00 PM

Mid-Night Check-In

Mid-hack review, pizza, energy boosters, and mini-challenges to keep the energy alive.

Day 2 — 10:00 AM

Submission Deadline

All projects must be submitted. Final 60 minutes of polishing, demos, and presentations prep.

Day 2 — 11:00 AM

Judging & Demos

Teams present their builds to the panel. Live demos, Q&A, and the ultimate showdown begins.

Day 2 — 02:00 PM

Closing & Prize Distribution 🏆

Winners announced, prizes distributed, and the CypherVerse champions are crowned forever.
